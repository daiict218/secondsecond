Introduction

The Jarga Ksetriya Vikas Smiti is a people's Organisations that was founded in 1996 in Kumbhalgarh Block, Rajsamand district, in Rajasthan. It is led and managed by poor people specially tribals in the area and is working in three blocks namely, Kumbalgarh, Naz-Tehsil Charbuja, Amet in Rajsamand and Gogunda block in Udaipur district of Rajasthan. Since it's inception, JSVS has been mobilizing poor people of the area to tacklesome burning problems of the area. From simple issues like drinking water facilities, proper running of schools, to related issues of land patta, land alienation, opposing moneylender, ramping exploitation in markets by upper cast and wide spread corrption. In addition, JSVS has led a major campaign against liquor, obscurantist practices and internecine feuds


It works under the following issues:

  • Land Related issue
  • Exploitation by money lender
  • Issues of Corruption
  • Dispute Resolution
  • Anti-liquor issue
  • Forest and revenue land issue
  • Education issue
  • Saving and credit
