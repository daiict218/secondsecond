TRIBAL RIGHTS RESOURCE UNIT


Vision And Thinking

  • SETTING UP PEOPLE'S ORGANISATION FOR POOR TRIBALS IN SOUTHERN RAJASTHAN SO THAT THE CAN WORK FOR THEIR DEVELOPMENT .
  • MAKING THESE ORGANISATION STRONG ENOUGH SO THAT THEY CAN WORK FOR LONG PERIOD OF TIME.
  • WHILE PRESERVING EXCLUSIVE CULTURE AND KNOWLEDGE OF TRIBAL PEOPLE,TRRU WANTS TO CONNECT THEM WITH SOCIETY.
  • TRRU WANT TO RAISE ISSUES RELATED TO TRIBAL PEOPLE UPTO NATIONAL LEVEL.

  • IN SEPTEMBER,1988 MORTGAGE JEWELS OF AROUND 1500 TRIBAL FAMILIES WORTH RS.40 LAKHS WERE RELIEVED
  • INCREAMENT IN WAGES OF 62000 TRIBAL FAMILY IN 1989(RS.10 TO RS.50)
  • WITH THE HELP OF COURT,ESTABLISHMENT OF A CEMENT FACTORY WAS STOPPED,WHICH IN TURN SAVED AROUND 292 ACRE OF FERTILE LAND OF TRIBALS,THUS GIVING THEM NEW HOPE TO LIVE.
  • 2400 TRIBAL FAMILIES OF AROUND 40 VILLAGES ARE GAINING BENIFITS FROM MINOR FOREST PRODUCTION FEDERATION
  • AROUND 367 GRAMSABHA WERE ORGANISED TO SOLVE THEIR DISPUTES,DEVELOPMENT AND TO SAVE FORESTS
  • INVOLVING UNDERAGE EMPLOYED BOYS AND GIRLS IN EDUCATION WHICH RESULTED IN INCREAMENT IN EDUCATION INDEX
  • ABOUT 600 MIGRANT WORKERS GOT WRITTEN CONTRACT REGARDING THEIR WORK, ADVANCE PAYMENT
  • 475 TRIBAL FAMILIES GOT THEIR MONEY(AROUND 55 Lakhs) BACK WHICH WAS SEIZED FROM THEM BY ILLEGAL MEANS.
