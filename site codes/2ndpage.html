Reach Astha Sansthan

Udaipur, also known as the City of Lakes, is a city, a Municipal Corporation and the administrative headquarters of the Udaipur district in the state of Rajasthan in western India.

The office of Astha Sansthan is situated in the Kharol colony, old Fatehpura of Udiapur.

The Udaipur City railway station is 15-20 minutes drive from Astha-Sansthan and the Udaipur Airport is a 35-40 minutes drive from Astha Sansthan.
